About Us

Langara College, located in the heart of Vancouver, has been at the forefront of education since its establishment in 1965. Over the years, our Web and Mobile App Development department has grown into a hub for aspiring tech professionals, focusing on equipping students with industry-relevant skills and a strong foundation in both theory and practical application.

Our core values are innovation, collaboration, and excellence. We believe in fostering a learning environment that encourages creativity, adaptability, and critical thinking, helping students to thrive in a rapidly changing digital landscape. By prioritizing hands-on experience and mentorship, we aim to prepare students not only to excel in the tech industry but to shape its future as well.
